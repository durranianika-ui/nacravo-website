## 11. Recommended URL strategy summary

| Ad intent | Final URL pattern | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Broad service | `/{service}` | `/specialized-cleaning` |
| Specific sub-service | `/{service}#{anchor}` | `/specialized-cleaning#sofa-cleaning` |
| Plumbing | `/handyman-services#plumbing` | (deep-links + preselects sub-service in the form) |
| AC chemical wash | `/ac-service-dubai#chemical-wash` | (preselects "AC Chemical Wash") |

**Why deep-link:** the page auto-preselects the matching sub-service in the lead form when the
`#anchor` is in the URL â€” so a "chemical wash" click lands with the form already set to AC
Chemical Wash. This is a real Quality Score and conversion advantage. **Do not** point final URLs
at the vanity redirects (`/sofa-cleaning`, `/plumbing`, etc.) â€” Google Ads dislikes redirects on
final URLs; use those only in the visible display path if desired.
