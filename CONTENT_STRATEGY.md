# Content Strategy — Nacravo Blog

**Prepared:** 21 July 2026
**Purpose:** the 11 commercial pages own **commercial** intent. The blog captures **informational**
intent (upper funnel) and feeds internal links and topical authority to the service pages. It must
not chase commercial keywords the landing pages already target — that would cannibalise them.

**Rules:** original, genuinely useful, Dubai-specific where relevant, no fabricated claims, no
keyword stuffing. Every post must earn its place under Google's Helpful Content guidelines and
link to at least one landing page.

---

## How to use this list

- **50 topics**, grouped by service, tagged by **search intent** and **funnel stage**, each
  **mapped to the landing page** it should link to.
- **Funnel stages:** TOFU (awareness/informational), MOFU (consideration), BOFU (near-purchase —
  used sparingly so as not to compete with landing pages).
- Prioritise TOFU/MOFU. BOFU posts should support, not duplicate, the landing pages.
- Publish cadence: 3–4 in Q1, then 2–3/month (see `SEO_ROADMAP_12_MONTHS.md`).

## Editorial guardrails

- **No cannibalisation:** blog posts target informational queries; landing pages keep the
  commercial ones ("AC service Dubai" stays a landing-page term, not a blog title).
- **Internal links are mandatory:** every post links to ≥1 landing page with descriptive anchor
  text.
- **Dubai-specific where it adds value:** humidity, sand dust, hard water, DEWA/Ejari, seasonality
  — not shoehorned.
- **No fabricated claims, ratings or prices** — same integrity rule as the site.
- **Refresh, don't abandon:** update the best performers quarterly rather than only publishing new.
