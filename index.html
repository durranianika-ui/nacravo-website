FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?+

No. Our team arrives fully equipped with professional, eco-friendly products. Materials are included in every cleaning price.

Will it be the same person each time?+

For Gold Club members, yes — the same named technician every visit, with a backup you've already met. For single bookings we send a trained member of our own team and tell you who's coming in advance.

Are your staff insured and verified?+

Yes. We're licensed and insured, and every technician is on our payroll, background-checked and trained to one standard — not dispatched from a marketplace.

How do I know the work was done properly?+

Every visit comes with before-and-after photos, timestamped, sent within the hour. If anything isn't right, we'll pull the photo report and re-do it at no charge.

Do you offer same-day service?+

Yes, subject to availability — and Gold Club members get same-day priority. Any urgency surcharge is always shown in your quote upfront.

Which areas do you cover?+

General cleaning and maintenance across Dubai, subject to availability. Premium AC servicing currently focuses on Downtown Dubai, Business Bay and DIFC. Concentrating our AC team on those districts is how we keep arrival times tight and quality consistent.
