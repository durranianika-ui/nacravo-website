What we clean, and how you can book it

Pick the format that suits your home and your schedule. Every option below is delivered by the same employed teams, with materials supplied.

Regular Cleaning

A recurring visit that keeps a Dubai home ahead of the dust. Technicians work a room-by-room checklist covering floors, kitchen surfaces, bathrooms, bedrooms and living areas. Fine sand settles quickly here, so skirting boards, window ledges and AC vent faces get attention as well. We keep the same team on your address wherever the schedule allows.

  • ✓Room-by-room checklist
  • ✓Floors, surfaces, bathrooms, kitchen
  • ✓Dust on ledges and vents
  • ✓Same team where possible

Steam Cleaning

High-temperature steam lifts grease and soiling without soaking a surface in chemicals. It suits tile grout, bathroom joints, kitchen splashbacks, mattresses and upholstery. Dubai humidity means fabrics need to dry properly, so technicians control moisture, ventilate the room, and tell you how long to leave the item before use.

  • ✓Grout, joints and splashbacks
  • ✓Mattresses and upholstery
  • ✓Low chemical use
  • ✓Drying time explained

FAQ

Home cleaning questions

Do I need to provide cleaning products?+

No. Materials and equipment are included and arrive with the technicians. If you prefer we use a specific product you already own, tell us at booking and the team will work with it. For low-fragrance or gentler products, ask for our eco option when you book.

Can I book the same cleaner each time?+

For recurring bookings we keep the same team on your address wherever scheduling allows, because a team that knows your home works faster. Leave, illness and route changes can occasionally require a substitute. When that happens, your agreed checklist goes with the replacement team.

Do I have to be home during the visit?+

No. Many customers leave access with building security or a concierge, or arrange key handover in advance. The before-and-after photo report exists precisely for this, so you can see the completed work without being present. Tell us your access method when you book.

What is the difference between regular and deep cleaning?+

Regular cleaning maintains a home that is already in reasonable condition. Deep cleaning tackles built-up grease, limescale, grout and areas a routine visit does not reach, and takes considerably longer. If a home has not been cleaned in some time, start with a deep clean, then maintain it weekly or monthly.

What are your working hours?+

We operate from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. You can pick any slot in that window, including weekends. Same-day bookings are possible subject to availability, but earlier notice gives you more choice of time and a better chance of your preferred team.

How do I book, and how is the price confirmed?+

Book by WhatsApp, phone or the form on this page. Describe the property and what you want covered, and we reply with a fixed price and a duration. Nothing is scheduled until you have that quote and agree to it.
