Handyman Jobs We Cover

Book one job or a list of them in a single visit. Tell us what needs doing and we will quote a fixed price before the technician starts.

Most handyman calls come with something else that needs doing. We cover AC servicing and repair in Downtown, Business Bay and DIFC, put recurring jobs on an annual maintenance contract, and handle the repairs that come up during a move-out inspection.

Plumbing Repairs

Dripping mixers, running cisterns, blocked kitchen and bathroom drains, loose traps and leaking flexible hoses. Dubai water heaters and concealed cisterns often fail at the seals first, so we check the whole run rather than only the visible drip. Any part is shown to you before it is fitted.

  • ✓Blocked sink and drain clearing
  • ✓Cistern and flush valve fixes
  • ✓Leak tracing and pipe sealing

Painting and Touch-Ups

We sheet the floor and furniture, prime the patched areas, then paint so the repair does not flash under the finished colour.

FAQ

Handyman Questions

Do you bring your own tools and materials?+

Yes. Our technicians arrive with the tools and equipment needed for the agreed job, and the materials for that scope are included in the quoted price. If you have already bought a specific bracket, paint colour or lock, we are happy to fit what you supply instead.

Can I book several different jobs in one visit?+

Yes, and it is usually the most economical way to use us. Send the full list on WhatsApp with photos where you can. We will tell you what can realistically be completed in one visit and quote the whole lot as a single fixed price.

Are your handymen employed or subcontracted?+

They are employed by Nacravo. We are not a marketplace that forwards your booking to whoever happens to be nearby that afternoon. Every technician is background-checked, uniformed, English-speaking and insured, and the company remains responsible for the work long after the visit itself has finished.

What are your working hours?+

We work from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM every day of the week. Early morning and evening slots are popular with residents who work full time, so book ahead where you can. Same-day visits are sometimes possible, subject to availability on the day you call.

Will I know the price before you start?+

Yes. We quote a fixed price based on your description and photos, and confirm it on site before any work begins. If the technician finds something different from what was described, we pause, explain the options and requote rather than continuing.

Do you send anything after the visit?+

Yes. We send a before-and-after photo report once the job is finished and the area has been tidied. Landlords, property managers and owners living outside the UAE find this particularly useful, as it gives them a dated record of what was repaired and how the property was left.
