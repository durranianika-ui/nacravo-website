FAQ

Pest Control Questions

Do I need to leave the property during treatment?+

It depends on the treatment. We tell you the required re-entry time before we attend, along with any preparation such as covering food, clearing surfaces or moving pet bowls and fish tanks. For most routine treatments the disruption is short, but you will always know in advance.

How should I prepare for a bed bug treatment?+

We send a written preparation list once the booking is confirmed. It typically covers stripping and laundering bedding on a hot wash, decluttering under and around the bed, pulling furniture away from walls, and clearing floor space so the technician can reach the harbourage points.

Why do cockroaches and ants keep coming back?+

Dubai's warmth and humidity keep activity going year round, and shared plumbing, drains and service ducts give pests a route back in. Treatment reduces the population, but sealing pipe collars, fixing drain traps and controlling food and water sources is what keeps them from returning.

Can you treat while my family is at home?+

Usually yes, with preparation. We explain which rooms need to be vacated and for how long, and we schedule around children, older residents and pets. If a treatment requires the property to be empty for a period, we tell you that before you book, not on the day.

Do you handle offices and restaurants?+

Yes. We treat offices, cafes, salons, clinics, retail units and storage areas, and can schedule visits around your trading pattern within our 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM working day. Recurring schedules are available, and every attendance comes with a photo report for your own records.

How quickly can you attend?+

Message us with the details and we will offer the earliest slot we have. Same-day attendance is sometimes possible, subject to availability. We work 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily, so evening appointments after work are often easier to get than midday ones.
