Specialist cleaning is often the finishing step. It pairs naturally with a full deep clean, with move-out cleaning when a landlord is inspecting upholstery, and with regular home cleaning once the heavy work is done.

Fabric sofas, armchairs and majlis seating cleaned by hot water extraction, with stains pre-treated before the machine work starts. We vacuum the frame and crevices first, then extract dirt from the fibres and leave the seating damp rather than soaked. Leather is cleaned and conditioned using a different method, so tell us which you have.

  • ✓Stains pre-treated by hand

Carpet Cleaning

Rugs, wall-to-wall carpet and runners shampooed and extracted in place. Dubai carpet collects fine sand dust deep in the pile that a domestic vacuum leaves behind, along with traffic marks in walkways and doorways. We pre-treat those areas, extract, and groom the pile so it dries evenly rather than in patches.

  • ✓Traffic areas pre-treated

We sanitise the surface, treat marks, and extract so the mattress dries as quickly as the room allows.

FAQ

Specialized Cleaning FAQs

How long does a sofa or carpet take to dry?+

Drying depends on the fabric, the thickness of the pile and the airflow in the room. Extraction removes most of the moisture, so items are left damp rather than wet. Running the air conditioning and keeping the room ventilated speeds it up, and we give you specific advice for the items we treated.

Will you remove every stain?+

We pre-treat stains by hand before machine work and most lift or fade noticeably. Some marks are permanent, particularly dye, ink, bleach and old spills that were rubbed in. We tell you before starting which marks we expect to improve and which are likely to remain visible afterwards.

Do you clean leather sofas?+

Yes, but leather is cleaned and conditioned rather than shampooed or extracted, because water-based methods can damage the finish. Tell us the seating is leather when you request a quote, and send a photo if you are unsure, so we bring the right products and quote the correct method.

Can curtains be cleaned without taking them down?+

In many cases yes. Curtains can be steamed and extracted on the rail, which avoids the take-down and rehang. Some fabrics and linings need off-site cleaning to avoid shrinkage or watermarking, so we check the material first and confirm which method applies before quoting.

Do you bring your own machines and water?+

Technicians arrive with the extraction and shampoo machines, cleaning materials and tools needed for the job, and these are included in the price. We use a water point at the property, so please make sure a tap and a power socket are accessible in or near the room.

Can you clean several items in one visit?+

Yes, and it is usually the better option. Sofas, carpets, mattresses and curtains can be treated in the same booking, which is more economical than separate visits. Send a list with photos and rough sizes and we will quote the whole set as one fixed price before scheduling.
