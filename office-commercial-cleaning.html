FAQ

Office & Commercial Cleaning FAQs

Can you clean outside our business hours?+

Yes. Most offices book us before opening, after closing or at weekends so staff are not working around equipment and wet floors. Our working hours are 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. Tell us your access arrangement, including any building sign-in process, and we will schedule inside it.

Do we need to supply cleaning materials?+

No. Cleaning materials and equipment are included in the price we quote, including machines for carpet shampoo where that is part of the scope. If you want us to refill washroom consumables you supply, we will check and top them up as part of each visit.

Are your cleaners screened before entering our premises?+

Yes. Technicians are employed by Nacravo rather than sourced from a marketplace, and they are background-checked, uniformed, insured and English-speaking. For contract work we keep the same team on your site so building security deals with familiar faces rather than a new person each visit.

How is a cleaning contract different from one-off bookings?+

A contract fixes the schedule, the team and the scope in writing, covering what is cleaned daily, weekly and monthly. One-off bookings suit deep cleans, post-event clearing or fit-out handovers. Many businesses run a daily contract and book deep cleaning or carpet shampoo separately when needed.

Can you clean an office after a fit-out?+

Yes. Fit-out cleaning removes builders dust from ceilings, ducts, glass and floors, and clears paint, adhesive and sticker residue before handover. It is usually staged as trades finish and snagging closes, so send us the programme dates and we will work to the handover deadline.

Do you handle emergency or same-day requests?+

Same-day attendance is possible subject to availability, which suits spills, post-event clearing or an inspection you were not expecting. Message us on WhatsApp with the site address, the access time and what needs doing, and we will confirm whether a team is free that day.
