FAQ

Holiday Home Cleaning FAQs

How quickly can you turn a unit around between guests?+

It depends on the size of the unit and what is included. A studio with a linen change is faster than a three-bedroom villa with restocking and an inspection. Send us the check-out and check-in times when you book and we will tell you whether the gap is workable before you confirm anything.

Do you provide the linen and towels?+

We change and make up the linen you supply, and strip used sets ready for your laundry provider. If you want us to arrange linen or consumables instead of supplying them yourself, mention it when you request a quote and we will include the cost in the fixed price we send you.

Can you clean while I am not in Dubai?+

Yes. Many hosts book turnovers remotely. Arrange access through the building, a lockbox or a key handover, and you will receive a before-and-after photo report after each visit, plus notes on anything damaged, missing or running low in the unit.

Do you handle multiple units on the same day?+

Yes, subject to availability. Send the list of addresses with the check-in time for each one and we will schedule them in an order that fits the windows. Hosts with several units usually set up a recurring arrangement so slots are held rather than requested each time.

What if the previous guest left damage?+

We photograph it and send it to you with the report rather than cleaning around it. That gives you dated evidence to raise with the booking platform or the guest. If the damage means the unit cannot be made guest ready in time, we tell you straight away.

Can you do a same-day turnover?+

Same-day is possible subject to availability. Our working hours are 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily, so late check-outs and evening check-ins can usually be covered. Message us on WhatsApp with the time window and we will confirm whether a technician is free.
