## 8. Review acquisition workflow

**No fake reviews. No incentivised reviews.** Both violate Google policy and risk suspension.

1. **Ask at peak satisfaction** — right after the before/after photo report is sent, which is
   already a touchpoint in the service flow.
2. **Ask on WhatsApp**, the channel the customer already uses, with the Google "write a review"
   **short link** so it is one tap.
3. **Personalise** — reference the specific job ("thanks for having us for the AC chemical wash
   today").
4. **Respond to every review**, positive and negative — response rate is a visible quality signal
   and negative-review handling builds trust.
5. **Target a stable base first** (20+), then steady monthly growth. Volume *and* recency matter.
6. **Never** buy reviews, gate them (only asking happy customers), or offer discounts for them.

Once a genuine corpus exists, activate `AggregateRating` schema on the site (see the review
strategy in `GOOGLE_ADS_READINESS_REPORT.md` §7) reflecting the **real** rating and count.
