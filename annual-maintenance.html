Preventive Maintenance

The point of an AMC is catching faults while they are still small. Blocked condensate drains, weeping isolation valves, failing silicone and loose electrical terminations all give warning signs long before they flood a ceiling or trip a circuit. We check the usual failure points on a set rota.

  • ✓Condensate drain clearing
  • ✓Valve, seal and sealant checks
  • ✓Termination and connection tightening
  • ✓Fault trends tracked between visits

FAQ

AMC Questions

What is included in an annual maintenance contract?+

The scope is agreed in writing for your property. It typically covers scheduled AC servicing, plumbing and drainage checks, electrical inspection and general repairs found during visits. Consumables for the scheduled work are included. Anything outside the agreed scope is quoted at a fixed price before it starts.

How often will you visit?+

Visit frequency is set in the contract and depends on the property and its systems. A small apartment needs fewer visits than a villa with several AC units, pumps and outdoor services. We propose a schedule after the site assessment and confirm each appointment with you before the day.

Do you offer emergency cover?+

Contract customers get priority callout response during our working hours of 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily, which means your property is placed ahead of general bookings. We do not offer round-the-clock cover, and we do not promise a guaranteed response time we cannot consistently meet.

Does an AMC cover chilled water systems as well as splits?+

Yes. We cover split systems and chilled water fan coil units, which is what most Dubai towers use. The service scope differs between them, so the assessment records what is installed. Chiller plant owned and operated by the building itself remains the responsibility of building management.

Can I take out an AMC for a property I rent out?+

Yes, and many of our contracts are landlord contracts. We can liaise with your tenant directly to arrange access, then send you the photo report after each visit. That gives you an ongoing record of the property's condition without needing to be in Dubai yourself.

What happens if a repair needs a new part?+

We show you the part and explain why it needs replacing before anything is fitted. The cost is quoted as a fixed price first. Nothing is replaced without your approval, and the before-and-after photo report documents the work once the repair is complete.
