2. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and able to enter into a binding agreement to book our services. By booking, you confirm that the information you provide is accurate and that you are authorised to request service at the property in question.

3. Service Availability

Nacravo provides residential and commercial cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, villa and office cleaning, post-construction and holiday-home cleaning, window cleaning, pest control, AC servicing, plumbing, electrical work, painting, handyman services and annual maintenance contracts. We currently focus on Downtown Dubai, Business Bay and DIFC. Services are subject to availability and to the scope confirmed in your quote. Our operating hours are Monday to Sunday, 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM (GST/UAE).

4. Bookings & Quotes

You can request a service by WhatsApp, telephone, email or our website form. We provide a fixed quote based on the details you give us, including property type, size and scope.
